Who We Are

About Tomoe Link

Tomoe Link is a Singapore-based consultancy specialising in integrated learning solutions, international engagement programmes, marketing and brand development services for organisations operating in an increasingly global and competitive environment.

We work closely with corporates, public entities and educational institutions to design and deliver high-impact initiatives that strengthen organisational capabilities, enhance stakeholder engagement, and support sustainable growth.

With a multidisciplinary approach that combines strategic communications, global exposure, experiential learning, and business advisory expertise, Tomoe Link serves as a trusted partner in helping organisations navigate transformation and unlock new opportunities across regional and international markets.

Our expertise extends beyond traditional training and educational services. We provide comprehensive strategic support in areas including leadership development, international study missions, corporate communications, public relations, brand positioning, and market engagement initiatives.

Backed by a strong regional and international network, we curate meaningful collaborations, cross-border learning experiences, and professionally managed programmes that create measurable outcomes and long-term value for our clients.

At Tomoe Link, we believe that successful organisations are built through continuous learning, strategic positioning, strong partnerships, and the ability to adapt confidently in a rapidly evolving global landscape.
